Our Story

Craftsmanship meets nature.

Located in Thunder Bay, I have spent over two decades mastering the hobby of aquascaping. My goal is to create natural, calm, and artistic environments that thrive. Whether it is a desktop mini-garden or a complex riparium, I focus on the craftsmanship of nature, ensuring every setup is both beautiful and biologically stable.

Services

Bespoke aquatic environments for your home or office.

Freshwater Aquariums

Custom-designed freshwater ecosystems tailored to your space. We handle everything from the initial biological cycling to livestock selection, creating a balanced underwater world that is easy to maintain and beautiful to watch.

Desktop Nano-Tanks

Compact, low-maintenance worlds perfect for home offices. These miniature aquascapes bring a sense of calm and focus to your workday without requiring large equipment or extensive space.

Ripariums & Terrariums

Unique semi-aquatic setups where plants grow above the waterline (Ripariums) or fully enclosed terrestrial moist environments (Terrariums). Perfect for growing rare orchids, mosses, and marginal plants.

Hardscape & Layouts

The 'skeleton' of your aquarium. We provide professional hardscaping services using premium materials like Seiryu stone, Dragon stone, and Manzanita wood to create depth, perspective, and flow before water is even added.

The Shrimp Room

While we do not currently sell fish, we specialize in breeding high-grade Neocaridina shrimp. These active, colorful cleaners are perfect for planted nano-tanks.

Why We Use Tissue Culture Plants

We exclusively use tissue culture plants to ensure your setup remains pest-free. Unlike potted plants, these are grown in sterile labs, guaranteeing no snails or algae are introduced to your new tank.
